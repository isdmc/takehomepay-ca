Takehome Pay Calculator

About This calculator is meant to estimate annual and per-paycheck take-home pay for a simple tax situation. Specifically, for Canadian workers who earn income from only a salary and are eligible for standard deductions and credits. It also aims to be clear in how take home pay is determined by optionally displaying intermediate tax, premium, deduction and credit amounts in addition to the final result.
